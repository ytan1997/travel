About Us

About

: Animal-based economy. A decentralized economy for animals based on blockchain technology. looks forward to giving animals, Vets, Owners, Charity and other animal care sectors, a common ground for transactions, data and resource management. This is a market, crowded with both care-givers and receivers. For the first time, animals are empowered financially through their participation on the platform. Here, the identity of animals, ownership, genealogy, health record and other related statistics are also available. Wildlife parks, zoos, and marine parks will be full partakers of this system.

What We Do

  • As we develop we believe in total integration and we aspire to develop teams around the world for animal tracing, registration and transfer of ownership and holistic healthcare.
  • We at V- Meow have a decentralized blockchain committed to the welfare of your animal companion with speedy contracts and information data.
  • Know your pet's and animal's history to provide you with fast and most effective treatment that can be given.
  • Our Apps and Dapps monitor marine, terrestrial and other ecosystems. These include zoos around the world, partnered with wildlife parks and animal reserves to use and share the Blockchain utility for storage and distribution of animal related records.
